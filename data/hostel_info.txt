Hostel Name: Etriq University International Hostel
Location: Ground Floor, Block A, University Campus.

Facilities:
- 24/7 Electricity and Water supply.
- High-speed Wi-Fi.
- Gym and Sports complex access.
- Laundry services available on Tuesday and Friday.
- Mess timing: Breakfast (8:00-9:30 AM), Lunch (1:00-2:30 PM), Dinner (8:00-9:30 PM).

Room Allotment Process:
1. Submit application at the Hostel Office or online portal.
2. Documents required: Admission letter, ID proof, Parent's consent form.
3. Payment of fees via the student portal.
4. Room key collection from the reception.

Rules:
- In-time for students: 10:00 PM.
- No outsiders allowed after 8:00 PM.
- Smoking and Alcohol are strictly prohibited.
- Maintenance issues should be reported at the reception desk.
